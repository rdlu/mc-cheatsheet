# Early game

The first few in-game days set up everything else. Rough priority order:
**wood → stone tools → food → bed → coal/iron → shield**.

## Day one

1. **Punch trees** — get ~8–12 logs. Craft: crafting table, wooden pickaxe.
2. **Go straight to stone** — mine 3 cobblestone, upgrade to a stone pickaxe,
   then stone axe/sword/shovel. Skip making more wooden tools.
3. **Food** — kill a few animals (cook the meat — raw food is a trap),
   or break tall grass for wheat seeds near a future farm spot.
4. **Bed** — kill 3 sheep (or shear with iron later) + 3 planks.
   Sleeping skips the night *and* sets your respawn. On a server,
   `gamerule playersSleepingPercentage 1` lets one sleeper skip for everyone.
5. **Shelter** — dig into a hillside and plug the hole. A hole with a door
   beats an ambitious half-finished house at sundown.

## First mining trip

- Take: stone pickaxe, sword, **food**, torches, a **water bucket** as soon
  as you have iron, and spare wood (craft tools/torches underground).
- Don't dig straight down (gravel, caves, lava) — staircase down instead.
- **Iron** is everywhere below sea level; ~32 ore makes full armor + tools
  with spare. Smelt with the coal you mine on the way.
- Lava lakes start around **Y −54**; diamonds are best at **Y −58/−59**
  (1.18+). Bring the water bucket before going that deep.
- Mark the way home: torches **on the right wall** going in — right-hand
  rule out.

## Early essentials checklist

- [ ] Full iron armor + shield (a shield blocks creeper blasts and skeleton arrows)
- [ ] Wheat/carrot/potato farm near base (hoe grass for seeds; villages have carrots/potatoes)
- [ ] Sugar cane on a water edge → paper later; **bamboo/cactus** spotted for future XP smelting
- [ ] Small cow/sheep pen (wheat lures them, breed pairs)
- [ ] Furnace array + chest organization before the hoard gets out of hand
- [ ] A **bucket** kept on the hotbar — water saves you from lava, falls, and creepers

## Worth knowing

- **Beds + village** — if a village is nearby, it solves food (farms),
  beds (houses), and a head start on [villager trading](villagers.md).
- **Spiders burn last** — at dawn, skeletons/zombies burn but creepers don't.
  Wait for full daylight or keep the shield up.
- **Tools**: don't enchant or anvil-repair iron gear — it's disposable.
  Save levels for the [enchanting table](enchanting.md) and diamond gear.
- **Death insurance**: until you trust your route, carry less. Items
  despawn 5 minutes after death (loaded chunks). Server-side rescue:
  `tp Alice <coords>` or temporary `gamerule keepInventory true`.
