## First farms, in order

1. **Crop farm** — 9×9 plot, water in the center hydrates the whole plot.
   Wheat + carrots + potatoes; bonemeal from a composter eating the excess.
2. **Sugar cane** — plant on sand/dirt next to water; only the bottom block
   needs to stay. Manual at first, observer-piston later.
3. **Cactus/bamboo smelter fuel** — both grow unattended; bamboo is also
   the classic furnace-XP afk farm.
4. **Mob XP farm** — dark room spawner (or a found dungeon spawner) with a
   water stream to a one-hit kill chamber. Skeleton spawners double as
   arrow + bone meal sources.
5. **Iron farm** — 3+ villagers + beds + a zombie scare → golems spawn into
   a kill chamber. Trivializes hoppers, rails, buckets, anvils.

## Notes

- Hostile mobs spawn at **light level 0** (1.18+) — torching to light 1+
  fully spawn-proofs an area.
- Mob farms only work when players are 24–128 blocks from the spawning
  spaces — build kill chambers with that band in mind.
- `gamerule mobGriefing false` **breaks** some farms (villager crop
  replanting still works, but creeper/wither-based designs don't).
