# Villagers & trading

*Seed page — to be expanded.*

## Why bother

Villagers convert renewable junk into the best items in the game:
**Mending books**, diamond gear, ender pearls, golden carrots. A small
trading hall replaces hours of mining and fishing.

## Basics

- A villager needs a **bed** and a **job site block** (lectern → librarian,
  blast furnace → armorer, grindstone → weaponsmith, …).
- **Librarian rerolling**: place/break the lectern until the novice offers
  the enchanted book you want (e.g. Mending) — the trade locks once you
  trade with them.
- Breeding: 3 bread or 12 carrots/potatoes per villager, plus spare beds.
- **Cure discounts**: a zombified villager cured with a splash weakness
  potion + golden apple gives near-permanent massive discounts (1-emerald
  Mending books). Stack-curing is the late-game move.

## Starter trades

| Villager | Trade worth grabbing |
| --- | --- |
| Farmer | crops → emeralds (easy emerald engine) |
| Librarian | Mending, Efficiency, Protection books |
| Armorer / Toolsmith / Weaponsmith | diamond gear for emeralds |
| Cleric | rotten flesh → emeralds; ender pearls |
| Fletcher | sticks → emeralds (cheapest emerald trade in the game) |
