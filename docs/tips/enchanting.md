# Enchanting & gear

*Seed page — to be expanded.*

## Setup

- Full enchanting table: **15 bookshelves** (1 block gap around the table)
  unlocks level-30 enchants. Shelves need 3 leather + 9 paper each — cows
  and sugar cane, or buy/loot books.
- Level 30 enchants cost **3 levels + 3 lapis** (not 30 levels) — enchant
  early and often once you're past level 30.

## Priorities

| Slot | First | Then |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pickaxe | Efficiency, Unbreaking | Fortune III (ore) / Silk Touch (2nd pick) |
| Sword | Sharpness | Looting III, Sweeping Edge |
| Armor | Protection IV | Feather Falling IV (boots), Respiration (helmet) |
| Everything | **Mending** | — from fishing, trading, or chest loot only |

## Anvil notes

- Combining two identical enchanted items merges enchants; the *prior work
  penalty* doubles each time — plan combines, don't chain endlessly
  ("Too expensive!" caps at 40 levels).
- Mending + XP farm = permanent gear; Mending and Infinity are mutually
  exclusive on bows.
- A grindstone removes enchants and refunds a little XP — useful for
  resetting a bad roll on a spare item.
