## Portal math

- 1 nether block = **8 overworld blocks**. Build nether-side hub tunnels and
  the overworld shrinks by 8×.
- To link portals deliberately: divide the overworld X/Z by 8, build the
  nether portal at those coordinates. Portals link to the **nearest existing
  portal** within range — misplaced portals cause "wrong exit" surprises.
- A portal frame needs only **10 obsidian** (corners can be anything) — a
  water bucket + lava lake casts a frame with zero diamond pickaxe.

## Survival notes

- **Fire Resistance potions** turn the nether from lethal to casual —
  brew with nether wart + magma cream.
- Bring: flint & steel (ghasts blow out portals), building blocks for
  bridging, a slab/cobble roof against ghasts on highways.
- Don't sleep — beds explode in the nether. (Respawn anchors work instead.)
- Hoglins fear warped fungus; piglins are neutral if you wear **any gold
  armor piece**.

## Fast travel

- Ice + boat: packed/blue ice boats hit ~40–70 blocks/s — the standard
  nether-highway design is a 2-wide blue-ice lane with a slab roof.
- Elytra + firework rockets is endgame travel; until then, nether hubs
  beat overworld roads.
